Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level

Calculates the Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level for English phrases to determine comprehension difficulty. The grades are based on the reading levels by school grade for the US school system. Low grades are best for targeting the general public. They are color coded for easy recognition.

  • Lime: grade 6 and below. Ideal level to use for spoken English, such as interactive voice response.
  • Yellow: grades 7-9. Good level to use for written English, such as documents and forms.
  • Red: grade 10 and above. Avoid using for the general public.

Formula

S = Sentences; W = Words; Y = Syllables;
